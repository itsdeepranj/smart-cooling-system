# System Overview

## System Name 

Smart Colling System for Power Inverter and DC-DC Converter

### Purpose

The Smart Colling System is designed to maintain the operating temperature of a power 
inverter and DC-DC converter within safe limits by monitoring thermal and coolant system 
parameters and controlling colling components such as pumps and fans.

## Application

Electrical vehicle or industrial power electronics systems.

### Primary Objective

Ensure the inverter and DC-DC converter temperature remains within in the 
safe operating range while optimizing energy consumption and protecting system components.
